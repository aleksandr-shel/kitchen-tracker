About Us


This T3 Kitchen Tracker will help you organize your refrigerator and pantry easily.

Searching and posting recipes has never been more simple.





The T3 Kitchen Tracker started with an idea from a group of software technology engineers desiring to

make a project that would be beneficial for busy individuals. Drawing from personal experience to

acknowledge that sometimes it is difficult to keep track of what is in your pantry and/or refrigerator.

This idea was expanded to include whole families, athletes, and professionals in the food industry.
